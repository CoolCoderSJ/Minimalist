Minimalist comes built in with badges support. If you would like to use a stylesheet for only badges, use

<link rel="stylesheet" href="https://badge.coolcodersj.repl.co/style.css">.

Classes

badge - The bottom-most badge. Badges has support for up to 4 badges.

badge2 - The second-to-last badge.

badge3 - The second-first badge.

badge4 - The topmost badge.

no-animate - Prevent image spin on hover.
